The pictures below are of my adventures in the woods this year. I love to see new places and my favorite new places to see are always in the forest or mountains. The beach is another special place that I love to go every chance I get. My first trip this year was to the Olympic National Forest in Washington. It is a three hour drive to it base. From there I drove along the eastern side of the forest to the Northern most point. The Olympic Nation Hot Springs was my first stop into the forest there. It was beautiful! Along with the perfect scenic drive there was an exciting four mile hike to the springs. We past by water falls and amazing mountain peak views before smelling the sulfer from the hot spring.

The second grand adventure I went on this year was to the Umpqua Hot Springs. As you can probably tell I like Hot Springs. This spring however is defferent from most; it is not heated due to sulfer. It is also special because of it's scenery! There are about eight different pools carved into the rock each one flowing into the next. They are placed just on the edge of a drop off above the beautiful Umpqua River. If you ever have the chance to visit the Umpqua Hot Spring I highly recomend it. Not only is the spring itself breath taking and relaxing but there is an amazing trail along the river that is a must see! another bonus is that Umpqua is located only 20-30 minutes away from Crater Lake!
