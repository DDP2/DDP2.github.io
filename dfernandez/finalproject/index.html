Biography

I designed the site the way I did, because I wanted it to be easy to navigate, purple, and simple yet able to look good on a mobile device. I am proud with how I styled my web journal, and how my rock, paper, scissors game turned out. The evolution of the site, is that is started out really bare, no navbar, or resizing was present. It was basically a boring website, but then I learned how to use bootstrap to customize my pages, and make it look more modern, and resizable. I was also able to better my game, by adding in a win / loss / tie counter, along with making it able to show the computer choice, and results on the page, instead of propping a alert that you had to submit.
