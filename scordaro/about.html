A Little Bit About Me

Sam Cordaro, the creator of this website, currently lives in Portland, Oregon with his family. He enjoys playing videogames, running, building computers, and hanging out with his friends. This is his first year coding in html and CSS, but he hopes to continue and create more advanced sites in the future.

I have a very poor eye for design, so there wasn't really a theme or look I was going for on my site. I just tried to lay it out as simply as possible, so that the website would be easy to navigate.
